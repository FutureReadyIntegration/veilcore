# Accessibility Engine

## Purpose

VeilCore includes an accessibility direction as a core platform concern.

Accessibility is not treated as decoration.
It is part of system capability.

---

## Current Direction

This includes:

- accessibility-aware platform design
- EqualShield
- Braille-oriented design goals
- operator usability improvements
- hospital accessibility consideration

---

## Why It Matters

Security platforms are often inaccessible.

That limits who can operate them and who can safely benefit from them.

In healthcare, accessibility is especially important.

---

## VeilCore Position

VeilCore aims to become a platform where accessibility is built in, not bolted on later.

This includes future validation of Braille-related functionality and broader accessibility operator support.

---

## Status

Accessibility engine work is active in concept and platform direction, with testing and validation still required in some areas.
